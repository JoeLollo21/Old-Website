Hi, I'm Joe Lollo.

I am a graduate student in the Master of Library and Information Science (MLIS) program at the University of Washington passionate about digital humanities scholarship and education, instructional design, and information access and usability.

Presently, I'm working as an Outreach and Communications Specialist with the UW Libraries, using my skills in communication and design to host public events for a variety of student and professional populations.

I graduated a B.A. in English and Media Studies from the UW Bothell campus in 2022, and I have written and spoken on the value of humanities education for public good in the past.

I use he/him and they/them pronouns interchangeably.
